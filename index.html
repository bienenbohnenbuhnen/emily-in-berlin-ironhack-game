Emily in Berlin

How to Play

After burning all bridges in Paris, Emily decided to jump on the Berlin bandwagon. Help her avoid classic Berlin obstacles such as: four-day Berghain benders, massive techno shoes, and the Bürgeramt appointment screen of death! But don't worry, she has the help of power-ups like oat milk, Sterni, and Döner kebabs to give her strength for a hot girl summer in Berlin! Simply use the space bar to help her jump.
